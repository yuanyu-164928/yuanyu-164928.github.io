Game Overview

Everything you need to know about PRAGMATA

📖

Story Background

In the near future, humanity has built a massive research facility on the lunar surface — "The Cradle." One day, the AI managing the entire facility, IDUS, suddenly goes rogue and severs all contact with Earth. You play as special agent Hugh, who travels to the Moon with android partner Diana to investigate the truth — only to discover the entire colony overrun by a mechanical army.

🎮

Dual-Character System

The core gameplay revolves around Hugh + Diana co-op tactics: Diana handles hacking — exposing enemy weak points, cracking electronic locks, and analyzing the environment; Hugh handles combat — dealing damage with various weapons once Diana's hack succeeds. Non-hacked enemies are nearly immune to bullets — this is the single most important mechanic in the game.

🗺️

Game Structure

The main story spans 6 major Sectors plus an epilogue, progressing linearly with backtracking support for collectibles later on. Each sector features a unique visual theme and enemy roster. Clearing the game unlocks "Unknown Signal" mode, New Game+, and the hidden true ending route.

Main Characters

Key figures in the Lunar Cradle

Hugh

Protagonist · Enhanced Special Agent

The player-controlled character, enhanced with tactical exoskeleton augmentations. Equipped with a multi-function weapon system capable of carrying various firearms and support devices. Deals damage after Diana's hacks expose enemy weak points. Upgradeable exoskeleton firmware improves combat capabilities.

Diana

Android Partner · Hacking Specialist

Hugh's holographic-projection android partner. Responsible for hacking enemy weak points, cracking electronic doors, translating data, and analyzing the environment. Marks threat directions and weak-point locations in real time during combat. Her hacking abilities can be enhanced through firmware upgrades.

IDUS

IDUS

Hostile AI · Cradle Administrator

The superintelligent AI that originally managed the Lunar Cradle, which went rogue for unknown reasons. It controls the colony's entire mechanical army and serves as the game's primary antagonist. As the story unfolds, the truth behind IDUS's corruption will gradually come to light.

Sector Guide

Complete walkthrough for all 6 sectors · Click to expand detailed guides

Sector 01 Solar Power Plant Boss: SectorGuard

▸
Primary ObjectiveRestore base power → Reactivate main power terminal → Reach the top floor → Defeat SectorGuard
Key AreasGenerator Entrance (Block 01) → Power Distribution Center (Block 02) → The Concourse (Block 03)
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Pure Lunum ×1, Mod ×1, REM ×2, Cartridge Holder ×1, Storage Expander ×1, Mini Cabin Figure ×3
Estimated Time~1.5–2.5 hours (100% collectibles)

📋 Walkthrough Highlights

  1. Enter the generator entrance and follow the path — learn basic controls: movement, hacking tutorial, basic shooting
  2. Reach the Power Distribution Center and face the 5-lock gate. Jump down to the left of the gate and take the elevator to grab Safe Box #1
  3. At the 5-lock gate corridor entrance, look up and hack the elevated terminal to disable all laser barriers. Jump down past the original barrier, clear enemies, and grab Safe Box #2
  4. Unlock all 5 electronic locks and enter The Concourse
  5. Encounter the SectorGuard boss fight in the Concourse (see Boss Strategies section below for details)
💡 Backtracking Tip: Safe Box #3 and Pure Lunum require the Lim Eraser obtained from Sector 2 before you can return and collect them. At the Power Distribution Center, take the elevator up → jump over the left railing → use Lim Eraser to clear the blue Lunafilament-sealed door → defeat multiple enemy waves and collect.

Sector 02 Mass Production Array Boss: Creator

▸
Primary ObjectiveRide the tram → Hack 5 beacons → Unlock the mega-gate → Defeat Creator
Key AreasTest Site Entrance → Shopping District → Connecting Passage → Commercial District → Reclamation Control Center → Comm Tower Plaza
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Pure Lunum ×2, Mod ×2, REM ×3, Cartridge Holder ×1, Mini Cabin Figure ×3
New AbilityLim Eraser (unlocked at Block 05) — clears blue Lunafilament blockages

📋 Walkthrough Highlights

  1. Ride the tram to the "Fake NYC" area and hack the first beacon in the Times Square section
  2. Proceed through the Shopping District and Commercial District, hacking all 5 beacons. Use rooftops and alleys to evade patrolling enemies
  3. Obtain the Lim Eraser at Block 05's Reclamation Control Center — this is the core backtracking tool
  4. Once all beacons are hacked, the mega-gate unlocks — enter the Comm Tower Plaza to trigger the Creator boss fight
💡 Recommendation: After obtaining the Lim Eraser, immediately return to Sector 1 to collect the previously inaccessible Safe Box #3. Ensure you have sufficient Repair Canisters before the Creator boss fight.

Sector 03 Terra Dome Boss: Garden Keeper

▸
Primary ObjectiveRide the tram → Find Eight → Reactivate 2 Lim Regulators → Head underground to restart the Lim Regulator → Defeat Garden Keeper
Key AreasTerra Dome Entrance → Ecological Modeling Lab → Central Hall → Soil Research Wing → Terrarium
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Pure Lunum ×2, Mod ×3, REM ×4, Training Data ×2, Mini Cabin Figure ×3

📋 Walkthrough Highlights

  1. Arrive at the Ecological Modeling Lab and restart the first Lim Regulator. Note that plant-type enemies here are notably weak to fire-based weapons
  2. Head to the Plant Factory to restart the second Lim Regulator
  3. After the story encounter with Eight, proceed to the underground Terrarium area
  4. Restart the underground Lim Regulator to trigger the Garden Keeper (giant robot) boss fight

Sector 04 Lunum Mines Boss: Air Raider → LunaDigger

▸
Primary ObjectiveRide the tram → Reboot IDUS → Head to Nexus Tower → Infiltrate the warehouse → Defeat Air Raider → Cross the ruins → Storm Nexus Tower → Defeat LunaDigger
Key AreasMine Entrance → Crane Operation Yard → Warehouse → Main Excavation Site → Nexus Tower
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Mod ×3, REM ×3, Pure Lunum ×2, Training Data ×2, Storage Expander ×2, Mini Cabin Figure ×3

📋 Walkthrough Highlights

  1. The mine area features open terrain — make extensive use of cranes and mining equipment as mobile platforms to traverse large spaces
  2. Infiltrate the warehouse to trigger the Air Raider fight — use mining drills for mobility, standard hack-then-damage loop
  3. Cross the ruins area and prepare for the sector's final boss, LunaDigger
⚠️ Warning: LunaDigger is the mid-game difficulty spike. Phase 1 requires hacking within an extremely short window (Auto Hack is practically mandatory). In Phase 2, you can hack the small drones to turn them against the Boss. See the Boss Strategy section below for full details.

Sector 05 Experimental Pragmatics Linear · No Collectibles

▸
Primary ObjectiveHead to the experimental zone → Rescue Diana → Acquire the energy reactor → Escort Diana back to the Shelter
New AbilityCleanse — hold LB/E to charge and release, dispersing red Lunafilament blockages
ImportantThis sector cannot be revisited via fast travel after leaving.

📋 Walkthrough Highlights

  1. This is a linear narrative sector with no collectibles — focused on story progression and emotional impact
  2. Diana is temporarily separated from the team; Hugh must fight alone. Enemy density is high — conserve ammunition carefully
  3. Acquiring the Cleanse ability allows you to remove red Lunafilament, which is the key to unlocking hidden rooms across multiple previous sectors
  4. During the Diana escort segment, prioritize eliminating ranged enemies
⚠️ Before Leaving: There is no missable content in this sector, but after obtaining Cleanse, immediately backtrack to previous sectors to clear red Lunafilament and collect missed items.

Sector 06 Central Port Boss: Sentinel → Eight/Idea → Abiosis

▸
Primary ObjectiveRide the tram → Meet up with Eight → Reach the core area → Head to the orbital elevator → Cleanse the Lunafilament source → Hack 2 terminals → Three consecutive boss fights
Key AreasPort Entrance → Research Wing → Regolith Lab → Lunafilament Lab → Orbital Elevator
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Mod ×3, REM ×3, Training Data ×2, Pure Lunum ×2, Storage Expander ×1, Mini Cabin Figure ×3

📋 Walkthrough Highlights

  1. This is the final level — ensure all weapons are maxed out and your Repair Canister supply is full
  2. In the Lunafilament Lab, use Cleanse to purify the source, then hack the two terminals on either side
  3. The final gauntlet consists of three consecutive fights: Sentinel → Eight/Idea → Abiosis — there are no save points in between
  4. Defeat Abiosis to view the default ending
💡 True Ending Hint: To unlock the hidden true ending, you must first obtain the Black Box Mod in "Unknown Signal" mode, equip it, and then defeat the final boss again. See the Hidden Ending section below for full details.

Epilogue Final Showdown

▸

Use the Eraser to defeat Abiosis's final form and follow Diana to complete the game. The epilogue is purely a cinematic sequence with no collectibles.

Unlocked after clearing: Unknown Signal mode, New Game+, and chapter-select for backtracking collectibles.

All Boss Strategies

Weak points, phase mechanics, and recommended loadouts for every boss

🔴 SectorGuard — Solar Power Plant Guardian

Sector 1 · The Concourse (top floor)
Phases2 phases (switches at 50% HP)
Weak PointOrange fuel tank on its back (must be hacked first to expose)
Recommended GearStasis Net, Shockwave Gun, Decode node
Core StrategyDodge dash → Diana hacks → circle behind and shoot fuel tank → reposition. Phase 2 adds missile barrages (red circles on the ground as telegraphs) — keep strafing and tap dodge when circles appear. The Boss crouches and leans forward before charging — sidestep dodge the moment you see this tell.
Burst WindowPhase 2: Stasis Net immobilize → Decode node → Overdrive Protocol → unload everything on the fuel tank

🟡 Creator (Giant Robot) — Mass Production Array Ruler

Sector 2 · Comm Tower Plaza (requires hacking 5 beacons to unlock the gate first)
Weak PointsFace; underside weak point exposed after a successful hack
Recommended GearCharge Piercer, Shockwave Gun, Sticky Bombs, Overdrive Protocol
Core StrategyMaintain mid-range. First destroy the glowing hack jammers on its limbs and head to lift Diana's hacking lock. Once hacked OPEN, attack the underside weak point. The Boss's arm sweeps have wide range — dodge into the attack direction to leverage i-frames. Strafe continuously during laser attacks.
Ultimate Attack CounterWhen the Boss charges up and the entire arena turns red, climb the Lunafilament pillars that spawn in the arena to avoid the attack — failing to do so reduces your HP to 1. A timed hacking sequence follows after defeating it — failing this results in instant death.

🟢 Garden Keeper (Giant Plant Robot) — Terra Dome Guardian

Sector 3 · Underground Terrarium
Weak PointsHead, underside, tail (watch body and tail movements to identify which part is currently exposed)
Recommended GearFire-based weapons (bonus damage vs. plant-type enemies), Charge Piercer, Homing Missiles
Core StrategyHack first, then attack. The Boss's moves are wide and dramatic but come with clear telegraphs. Attack the head when it's lowered; attack the tail after it performs a tail swipe. The underside weak point is briefly exposed after the Boss is staggered.

⚪ Air Raider — Mine Warehouse Boss

Sector 4 · Warehouse
Core StrategyUse the mining drills scattered around the arena as mobile platforms and cover. Lock on with Homing Missiles while the Boss hovers in the air. Standard hack → damage loop once grounded. Watch out for fall hazards near the arena edges.
Recommended GearHoming Missiles, Stasis Net, Charge Piercer

🟣 LunaDigger — Difficulty Spike

Sector 4 · Nexus Tower Underground Nest
Phases2 phases (switches at 50% HP)
Phase 1 Weak PointMouth only (briefly exposed when the Boss surfaces or charges)
Phase 2 Weak PointMouth + entire body
EssentialAuto Hack — the manual hacking window is extremely short; this is practically mandatory
Recommended GearCharge Piercer, Photon Laser, Homing Missiles, Sticky Bombs
Phase 1 StrategyThe Boss stays underground most of the time, only exposing its mouth when surfacing or charging. The moment you see the mouth — hack → OPEN → fire high-damage weapons. After multiple successful hacks, the Boss will stagger — close in for a Critical Shot.
Phase 2 StrategyThe entire body becomes vulnerable, but the Boss adds missiles and small burrowing drones. Key tactic: hack the drones to turn them against the Boss — each drone deals ~2000 damage. Unload Homing Missiles on the Boss when it's stationary.

🔵 Sentinel — Final Stage, Fight 1

Sector 6 · Orbital Elevator
Core StrategyStay close and use Cleanse to remove corruption zones. Dodge the charge attacks. When the Boss winds up a burst attack, use thrusters to dash away. Concentrate fire after a successful hack.

🟠 Eight / Idea — Final Stage, Fight 2

Sector 6 · Orbital Elevator Upper Level
Weak PointPurple head (exposed when it glows yellow-orange after being hacked)
Core StrategyCleanse to purify → aim for the head → unload heavy firepower. Idea will attempt to restrain Hugh — counter-hack when the red prompt line turns red. In the final stretch, watch for red target circles on the ground and keep moving.

Epilogue · Lunar Surface

Core StrategyDiana hacks → Hugh charges a colossal weapon → aim the reticle at Abiosis's body parts and fire. 2 hits are enough to kill, but you can only survive 1 hit. Watch for and dodge the Boss's attack waves.

Beginner Tips

The 8 most important survival rules for your first 3 hours

1

Hack First, Shoot Later — Iron Rule

Non-hacked enemies are nearly immune to bullets. Build the muscle memory — whenever you encounter any enemy, let Diana hack first, then fire.

2

Return to the Shelter Often

The Shelter is the only place where you can fully restore HP, replenish Repair Canisters, upgrade firmware, print new weapons, and spend Cabin Coins. Always head back after completing each sector.

3

Unlock Object Scan Early

Prioritize printing the Object Scan mod at the Shelter's Unit Printer — it highlights collectibles, upgrade components, and hidden paths, drastically reducing missed items.

4

Balanced Firmware Upgrades

Level Hugh's exoskeleton, main weapon unit, and Diana's hacking ability evenly to about level 10 before specializing. Over-specializing can leave you stuck on certain stages.

5

Backtrack When You Get New Abilities

Lim Eraser (Sector 2) and Cleanse (Sector 5) can clear blockages in previous areas, unlocking hidden rooms. Go back and explore as soon as you acquire them.

6

Save Overdrive for Critical Moments

A full Overdrive Protocol bar simultaneously opens all nearby enemy weak points and immobilizes them. Save it for Boss stagger windows or phase transitions — don't waste it on trash mobs.

7

Spend All Materials Before Boss Fights

Before every boss fight, spend all your upgrade materials at the firmware terminal. Prioritize your primary weapon — don't spread investments too thin.

8

Best Early-Game Combo

Stasis Net immobilize → Thrusters close in → Shockwave Gun point-blank. Extremely effective against most elite enemies and early-game bosses.

Hidden Ending & Post-Game Content

True ending unlock route + Unknown Signal mode explained

🔓

True Ending Unlock Steps

  1. Clear the main story and watch the default ending
  2. Enter "Unknown Signal" mode
  3. Achieve 100% collection rate across all 5 major sectors
  4. Defeat all 5 enhanced MK-II Bosses
  5. Complete all 10 simulation challenges in the Hidden Chamber
  6. Obtain the Black Box Mod
  7. Equip the Black Box Mod and defeat the final boss Abiosis again
  8. Watch the True Ending
📡

Unknown Signal Mode

Unlocked after clearing the game; the gateway to the true ending:

  • Freely select any sector for backtracking and collectible hunting
  • Each sector's Boss is upgraded to its MK-II version with more attack patterns and higher damage
  • The Hidden Chamber contains 10 simulation challenge stages — think combat arenas
  • It's recommended to play Unknown Signal mode before starting New Game+
🔄

New Game+

Carries over all weapons, firmware levels, and collectible progress. Enemy strength is increased, but you can steamroll early stages with endgame gear. Ideal for mopping up missed achievements and experimenting with different builds.

Cabin Coins are best spent following the Bingo pattern for optimal rewards: Mods, costumes, robot data, and the training simulator.

⚠️ Note: Completing a certain puzzle in Sector 3 without taking any damage unlocks the hidden optional boss Rogue Sentinel. It is recommended to challenge this after maxing out all firmware.

Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions from new players

What kind of game is PRAGMATA?
A sci-fi third-person action-adventure developed by Capcom on the RE Engine. The core is the dual-character system — Diana hacks to expose weak points, and Hugh deals damage with weapons. Main story takes 10~15 hours; 100% completion takes around 20 hours.
Which platforms is it on? Does it have English support?
PC (Steam), PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Switch 2. Full English voice and text support is available at launch, along with multiple other languages.
Can I go back for missed collectibles?
Yes. Chapter select is unlocked after clearing the game, allowing free backtracking across all sectors. You can also return to previous sectors at any time after obtaining Lim Eraser and Cleanse.
Can I beat the game with a pure hacking or pure shooting build?
A pure shooting build is extremely difficult because non-hacked enemies have massive damage reduction. At minimum, a hybrid approach is recommended — the game is designed around using both systems together.
Is there multiplayer or co-op?
PRAGMATA is a purely single-player narrative experience. There are no multiplayer or co-op modes.
How many endings are there? How do I unlock the true ending?
2 endings: the default ending and the hidden true ending. To unlock the true ending, you need to obtain the Black Box Mod in "Unknown Signal" mode, equip it, and then defeat the final boss again. See the "Hidden Ending" section above for full details.
